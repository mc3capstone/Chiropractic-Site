David Goodman
Owner/Experienced Chiropractor

When I was attending Temple University, I realized that I wanted to be physically interactive with people, and help them feel better.

... read more

When I was exposed to chiropractic, I was amazed at how my body felt energized. It was a long road as I moved to Marietta, Georgia, right outside Atlanta and spent 5 years there. It was an amazing experience in so many ways. I moved home from Georgia, and practiced for a year at a friend’s practice in a gym atmosphere. Although I didn’t bulk up the way I intended at the gym, it laid the foundation for opening Goodman Family Chiropractic in 2001. Besides the chiropractic experience in my life, I have so many things to be thankful for. Foremost, my life with my wife, Cecille. Married since 2003, we’ve been blessed with a daughter in 2009, and expecting another as we are involved in an international adoption by 2015. We are flabbergasted!

In my spare time, I am an active, professional musician playing drums and percussion, brief attempts at guitar and singing.

Our family tries our best to live a healthy, active lifestyle. In between all the fun, daddy stuff and the band, I'm an avid gym goer with racquetball and spin classes in the forefront. This directly leads me to get to my chiropractor ASAP! How many hours are in the day?

I look forward to meeting you on your journey to feeling your best!
